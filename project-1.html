Project 1

The Financial Markets Dealers Association of Nigeria (FMDA) formerly known as Money Market Association of Nigeria (MMAN) is a self regulatory body in Nigeria; initially registered in 1989 under land (Perpetual succession) Act of 1962 and registered later under the Incorporated Trustees on 11th March 2010 as FMDA; to build capacity, identify, support and develop, where necessary possible financial market infrastructure, human capital and promote professional and ethical standards in treasury activities in Nigeria.

Live Link

Project Overview

The purpose of this web application is to automate the day-to-day activities of the financial market dealers association which includes but not limited to membership unboarding,Payments,Event's announcement and registration etc I Implemented a scalable and maintainable API to ensure this application is de-coupled(i.e we have a separate technology to handle our client and server's side of the application respectively). We also Implement a real-time chat functionality using WebSocket technology, this is to enhance communication and collaboration among FMDA members on the FMDA website.

Also, we implemented a forum place for the fmda people in accordance with their organization itenery and structure.
